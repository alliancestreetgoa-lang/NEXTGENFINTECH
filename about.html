Our Story

One firm, five disciplines

NextGen FinTech was founded by Aniruddha Roy — a rare multi-qualified professional holding Chartered Accountant (CA), Cost & Management Accountant (ICWA), Company Secretary (CS), and Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) credentials, alongside a B.Com. That combination means finance, costing, law, and investment thinking sit under one roof, in one relationship.

We started the firm on a simple conviction: most businesses don't need more spreadsheets, they need a partner who connects the dots. Compliance, profitability, and funding are not separate problems — they're one continuous story, and we help you tell it well.

Today we work with startups, MSMEs, and established enterprises across South Goa, combining the rigor of a traditional practice with the responsiveness and tooling of a modern one.
